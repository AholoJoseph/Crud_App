PLEASE FOLLOW THE INSTRUCTIONS BELOW TO MAKE THIS CRUD(Create,Retrieve,Update and Delete) APPLICATION WORK. 

1.Make sure all required Maven depedencies are installed in your IntelliJ IDEA or else please download the required Maven dependecies.
2.Connect to your localhost server(using Xamp or Ammp) in phpmyadmin and create a database("students").
3.Import the table("register") which is found in the project folder into the database you have created.
4.RUN the application and it will work perfectly.
